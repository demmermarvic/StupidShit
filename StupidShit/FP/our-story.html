Our Story

At Perfect Threads, we specialize in creating high-quality, custom-tailored garments that cater to the diverse needs of our clients. One of our signature services is the *baston*, a finely tailored shirt or suit made from premium fabrics, ideal for formal occasions or those seeking a sharp, well-fitted look.

We also offer *putol* services, where we modify existing clothing to ensure a better fit. Whether you need to shorten trousers, adjust sleeve lengths, or reshape dresses and suits, we make sure your garments not only fit comfortably but also enhance your personal style. Our alteration services are tailored to meet the individual needs of our clients, ensuring every piece is perfectly fitted.

With our *balik original* service, we restore and refurbish old garments, bringing them back to life while preserving their sentimental value. Whether it's a vintage suit or a beloved dress, we specialize in revitalizing garments to look and feel like new, using the same meticulous craftsmanship that went into their creation.

In addition, we create custom school uniforms designed for both comfort and durability. We understand the importance of well-fitted uniforms, and our tailoring ensures that students have clothing that meets school specifications while providing a perfect fit. Our uniforms are made with high-quality materials to ensure long-lasting wear throughout the school year.

At Perfect Threads, we combine traditional tailoring techniques with modern styles to meet the ever-evolving needs of our customers. Whether it's for a formal occasion, everyday wear, or a simple alteration, we are dedicated to providing exceptional service and garments that our clients can be proud to wear.
